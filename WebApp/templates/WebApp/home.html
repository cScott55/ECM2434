Group Software Engineering Project: Green Beans!

Hello!

Welcome to Green Beans! This a group developed web app with the goal of promoting sustainability and eco friendly

practises at the University Of Exeter. As part of the app, users will get their very own Green Bean Pet, which

they can feed by completing challenges. These challenges range from walking to recycling, all which allow users

to gain points and climb the leaderboard. See if you and your Green Bean can reach the top!

Our Goals

Our goal is to help promote sustainable practises on the University of Exeter campus. The university aims by 2030

to have a carbon net zero impact, and we all can easily help achieve this in our everyday lives. We wish to show

how a few small changes can have a big impact on the environment, whether it be walking instead of driving or

using a recyling bin instead of a regular one. We also want to encourage users to explore the University campus

with location based challenges that help show off some natural beauty. The best part is you get to do it alongside

a cute little green bean pet!

How Do I Start?

To get your own pet, register an account with us and then you can get straight into the challenges!
